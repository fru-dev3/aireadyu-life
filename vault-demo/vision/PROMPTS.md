# AI Ready Life: Vision — Prompt Reference

## Monthly Scorecard
- "Run my monthly scorecard"
- "Score my life domains"
- "What is my overall life score this month?"
- "How am I doing across all domains?"
- "Build my monthly vision brief"

## Quarterly Planning
- "Draft my Q2 quarterly plan"
- "Run my quarterly planning session"
- "What OKRs should I set for this quarter?"
- "Review last quarter's OKR progress"
- "Set my quarterly objectives"

## Annual Review
- "Run my annual review"
- "How am I tracking against my annual goals?"
- "What have I accomplished this year?"
- "What annual goals are at risk?"
- "Am I aligned with my annual theme?"

## Goal Tracking
- "How are my goals progressing?"
- "Flag any stalled goals"
- "Which goal needs the most attention right now?"
- "Log a milestone: [goal, what happened, date]"
- "What quarterly goals are stalled?"

## Domain Scoring
- "Score my health domain"
- "Score my wealth domain"
- "Score my career domain"
- "What domain has the lowest score?"
- "What domain improved the most this month?"

## Values Alignment
- "Does my current focus align with my values?"
- "What decisions should I make that align with [value]?"
- "Am I living in alignment with my stated values?"

## Strategy
- "What should I focus on this quarter?"
- "Am I spending time on the right domains?"
- "What is my biggest opportunity this quarter?"
- "Build my domain progress scorecard"

## Open Loops
- "Update my vision open loops"
- "What vision action items are unresolved?"
