# AI Ready Life: Estate — Prompt Reference

## Monthly Reviews
- "Run my estate monthly review"
- "Give me my estate brief"
- "What estate actions do I need to take this month?"

## Portfolio Summary
- "Build my portfolio summary"
- "What is my total portfolio value?"
- "What is my total equity across all properties?"
- "What is my portfolio cash-on-cash return?"

## Cash Flow
- "Analyze my cash flow"
- "Build my cash flow summary"
- "What is my net operating income?"
- "How much cash flow am I generating per property?"
- "What is my monthly cash flow after all expenses?"

## Tenants
- "Review my tenant status"
- "Which leases are expiring soon?"
- "What is the status of rent collection this month?"
- "Flag any lease renewals coming up in 90 days"
- "Run my tenant review"

## Maintenance
- "Build my maintenance schedule"
- "What maintenance is coming up?"
- "Log a maintenance expense: [property, amount, description]"
- "What have I spent on maintenance this year?"
- "Flag any overdue maintenance items"
- "Run my maintenance review"

## Insurance
- "When does my landlord insurance renew?"
- "Am I adequately covered on all properties?"
- "Review my estate insurance details"

## Financials
- "What is my cap rate on [property address]?"
- "Am I hitting my target cash-on-cash return?"
- "Log an expense: [property, amount, category]"
- "What is my depreciation schedule?"

## Open Loops
- "Update my estate open loops"
- "What estate action items are unresolved?"
