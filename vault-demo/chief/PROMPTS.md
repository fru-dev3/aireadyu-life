# AI Ready Life: Chief — Prompt Reference

## Daily Brief
- "Run my daily brief"
- "What needs my attention today?"
- "Give me my morning brief"
- "What is the most urgent item across all my domains?"
- "Brief me on the top 3 things I should do today"

## Weekly Preview
- "Build my weekly agenda"
- "What's coming up this week across all domains?"
- "Run my weekly preview"
- "What deadlines are due this week?"
- "What should I be focused on this week?"

## System Health
- "Run my system health check"
- "Which domains are behind on reviews?"
- "Are all my domain states current?"
- "Which plugins need attention?"

## Open Loops
- "Check all open loops across all domains"
- "What items are unresolved across my life?"
- "What has been open for more than 2 weeks?"
- "Pull open loop status from all domains"

## Cross-Domain Alerts
- "Collect alerts from all domains"
- "What is flagged as urgent across my domains?"
- "Are there any critical items I'm missing?"
- "What should I not let fall through the cracks this week?"

## Domain Status
- "Pull status from all active domains"
- "What is my health status summary?"
- "What is my financial status summary?"
- "Check status across health, wealth, tax, and career"

## Priorities
- "Am I focused on the right priorities this quarter?"
- "How are my top priorities progressing?"
- "What quarterly goal is most at risk?"

## Review Brief
- "Run my chief review brief"
- "Give me a cross-domain summary"
- "What's the overall state of my life system?"
