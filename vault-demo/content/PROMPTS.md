# AI Ready Life: Content — Prompt Reference

## Weekly & Monthly Reviews
- "Run my content weekly review"
- "Run my content monthly review"
- "Give me my content brief"
- "How is my content business performing this month?"

## YouTube
- "How is my channel performing?"
- "What are my top videos by views this month?"
- "What is my subscriber growth trend?"
- "Analyze my YouTube analytics"
- "What topics are performing best?"

## Newsletter
- "What is my newsletter open rate?"
- "How are my newsletter subscribers trending?"
- "What was the best-performing newsletter issue?"
- "How many subscribers did I add this week?"

## Revenue
- "Build my revenue summary for this month"
- "What is my total content revenue?"
- "Log YouTube revenue: [amount, period]"
- "Log Gumroad revenue: [amount, product]"
- "What is my revenue by channel?"
- "How am I tracking against my monthly revenue goal?"

## SEO
- "What are my SEO gaps?"
- "Run my SEO review"
- "What keywords should I be targeting?"
- "Build my SEO summary"
- "Flag any SEO opportunities I'm missing"

## Publishing Schedule
- "Am I on track with my publishing schedule?"
- "What content is due this week?"
- "Review my content calendar"
- "What should I publish next?"

## Analytics
- "Analyze my channel performance"
- "Build my analytics summary"
- "What content is driving the most conversions?"
- "Which channel has the best engagement rate?"

## Open Loops
- "Update my content open loops"
- "What content action items are unresolved?"
