# AI Ready Life: Brand — Prompt Reference

## Monthly Reviews
- "Run my monthly brand review"
- "Build my brand analytics summary"
- "Give me my brand brief"
- "What are my brand growth trends this month?"

## Analytics
- "Summarize my YouTube analytics"
- "What is my newsletter open rate?"
- "How are my LinkedIn followers trending?"
- "Build my analytics summary across all channels"
- "Which content drove the most engagement this month?"

## Profile Audit
- "Audit my profile consistency across channels"
- "Are my bio, headshot, and handles consistent everywhere?"
- "Check my profile for inconsistencies"
- "Run my brand profile audit"
- "Flag any profile inconsistencies"

## Mentions & Monitoring
- "Are there any brand mentions this week?"
- "Analyze recent mentions of [brand keyword]"
- "Log a new brand mention: [source, content, date]"
- "What are people saying about [my brand/company/topic]?"

## Content Strategy
- "What content is performing best?"
- "What topics should I create next based on my analytics?"
- "Am I publishing consistently?"
- "Review my content performance"

## Growth
- "What is my month-over-month newsletter growth?"
- "Am I on track for my subscriber goal?"
- "Which channel is growing fastest?"
- "What should I focus on to grow my brand this quarter?"

## SEO & Discoverability
- "What keywords am I ranking for?"
- "What brand keywords should I own?"
- "How is my Google Search Console performing?"

## Open Loops
- "Update my brand open loops"
- "What brand action items are unresolved?"
