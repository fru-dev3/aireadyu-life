# AI Ready Life: Intel — Prompt Reference

## Daily & Weekly Briefings
- "Run my daily briefing"
- "Give me my morning brief"
- "What happened in [topic] this week?"
- "Run my intel review"
- "Summarize today's top stories for me"

## Source Scan
- "Scan my sources for news on [topic]"
- "Run my source scan"
- "What's trending in [industry]?"
- "Pull stories from [specific source]"

## Topic Deep Dives
- "Give me a deep dive on [topic]"
- "Run a topic deep dive on [company/trend/event]"
- "Summarize everything that's happened with [topic] this month"
- "What's the context behind [news story]?"

## Filtering & Priorities
- "Flag any priority stories from today's briefing"
- "What stories match my priority keywords?"
- "Filter this briefing for only AI and personal finance stories"
- "What should I actually pay attention to this week?"

## Sources
- "Log a new source: [name, url, type]"
- "Review my saved sources"
- "What sources should I add for better [topic] coverage?"

## Saving & Archiving
- "Save this story to my archive"
- "What stories did I save last week?"
- "Archive this digest"

## Companies & People
- "What is happening with [company] right now?"
- "What has [person] said or done recently?"
- "Any news about [company] earnings or announcements?"

## Open Loops
- "Update my intel open loops"
- "What intel action items are unresolved?"
