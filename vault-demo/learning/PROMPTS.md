# AI Ready Life: Learning — Prompt Reference

## Monthly Reviews
- "Run my learning monthly review"
- "Give me my learning brief"
- "What should I focus on learning this month?"
- "Review my learning progress"

## Course Progress
- "Am I on track with my courses?"
- "What is my progress in [course name]?"
- "Build my course progress summary"
- "Which course should I prioritize this week?"
- "Flag any courses I'm falling behind on"

## Reading
- "Build my reading progress summary"
- "What books am I currently reading?"
- "What's on my reading list?"
- "Log a book completion: [title, author]"
- "What should I read next?"

## Skills Gap
- "What skills should I be developing for my target role?"
- "Run my skills gap analysis"
- "What am I missing to qualify for [target title]?"
- "Build a learning roadmap for becoming a [role]"

## Certifications
- "What certifications am I working toward?"
- "What is my progress on [certification]?"
- "When is my certification exam scheduled?"
- "Flag any certifications I should prioritize"

## Learning Goals
- "Run my learning goal review"
- "Am I hitting my weekly learning hour goal?"
- "What is my learning progress for this quarter?"

## Log & Complete
- "Log a course completion: [name, platform]"
- "Log a book I just finished: [title, author]"
- "Add a course to my wishlist: [name, platform]"

## Employer Benefits
- "What learning benefits does my employer offer?"
- "Have I used my tuition reimbursement this year?"

## Open Loops
- "Update my learning open loops"
- "What learning action items are unresolved?"
