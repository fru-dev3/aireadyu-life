# AI Ready Life: Home — Prompt Reference

## Weekly & Monthly Reviews
- "Run my home weekly review"
- "Run my home monthly review"
- "Give me my home brief"
- "What home items need my attention this week?"

## Maintenance
- "Build my home maintenance schedule"
- "What maintenance is coming up this month?"
- "What seasonal maintenance should I do this [season]?"
- "Flag any overdue maintenance items"
- "Log a maintenance item: [description, date, cost]"
- "When was my HVAC last serviced?"

## Seasonal Checklists
- "What should I do to prepare my home for winter?"
- "What spring home maintenance tasks should I tackle?"
- "Build my fall home checklist"
- "What summer home tasks are coming up?"
- "Run my seasonal maintenance review"

## Expenses
- "Build my home expense summary"
- "How much have I spent on home maintenance this year?"
- "Log a home expense: [amount, category, description]"
- "Am I within my maintenance budget?"
- "Review my home expenses"

## Contractors
- "Who is my plumber?"
- "Get me the contact for my HVAC contractor"
- "Add a new contractor: [trade, name, phone]"
- "Who did I use for [repair type] last time?"

## HOA
- "What are my HOA dues?"
- "When is my HOA payment due?"
- "What does my HOA cover?"

## Open Loops
- "Update my home open loops"
- "What home action items are unresolved?"
