# AI Ready Life: Social — Prompt Reference

## Monthly Reviews
- "Run my relationship review"
- "Run my social monthly review"
- "Give me my social brief"
- "How are my relationships doing?"

## Outreach Queue
- "Build my outreach queue for this week"
- "Who is overdue for a check-in?"
- "Who should I reach out to today?"
- "Flag any relationships that are overdue"
- "Show me my overdue close contacts"

## Birthdays
- "Who has a birthday coming up?"
- "Any birthdays in the next 30 days?"
- "Run my birthday watch"

## Relationship Health
- "Build my relationship health summary"
- "How is my close tier doing?"
- "Which relationships need the most attention?"
- "Am I maintaining my strong-tier contacts?"

## Outreach & Messaging
- "Draft an outreach message to [name]"
- "Help me reconnect with [name] — we haven't talked in 6 months"
- "Write a birthday message for [name]"
- "Draft a coffee chat invite for [name]"

## Logging
- "Log an interaction with [name]: [date, what we discussed]"
- "I just caught up with [name] — log that"
- "Update the last contact date for [name]"

## Contacts
- "Add a new contact: [name, relationship, check-in interval]"
- "Who is in my close tier?"
- "How many contacts do I have in each tier?"

## Goals
- "Am I hitting my monthly outreach goal?"
- "How many coffee chats have I had this month?"

## Open Loops
- "Update my social open loops"
- "What relationship action items are unresolved?"
