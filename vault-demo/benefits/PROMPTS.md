# AI Ready Life: Benefits — Prompt Reference

## Weekly & Monthly Reviews
- "Run my benefits review"
- "Give me my benefits brief"
- "What benefits actions do I need to take this month?"

## 401(k)
- "Review my 401k allocation"
- "Am I contributing enough to get my full employer match?"
- "What is my employer match formula?"
- "How much have I contributed to my 401k this year?"
- "Should I be contributing to Roth or traditional 401k?"
- "Run my 401k review"

## HSA
- "Check my HSA balance and contributions"
- "Am I on track to max my HSA this year?"
- "What is the HSA contribution limit this year?"
- "How much has my employer contributed to my HSA?"
- "Run my HSA review"

## Health Coverage
- "Build my coverage summary"
- "What is my health insurance deductible?"
- "What is my out-of-pocket maximum?"
- "What does my plan cover for [specific service]?"
- "Review my health insurance details"

## ESPP
- "Am I enrolled in ESPP?"
- "What is my ESPP discount percentage?"
- "When is my next ESPP purchase date?"
- "What is the guaranteed return on my ESPP contribution?"

## Equity Grants
- "What equity grants do I have?"
- "What is my vesting schedule?"
- "How much equity has vested?"
- "What is the total value of my unvested equity?"

## Open Enrollment
- "Is open enrollment coming up?"
- "Flag if my open enrollment window is approaching"
- "What should I review before open enrollment?"
- "What changes should I make during this year's enrollment?"

## Open Loops
- "Update my benefits open loops"
- "What benefits action items are unresolved?"
- "Extract my current coverage limits"
