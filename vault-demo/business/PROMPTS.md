# AI Ready Life: Business — Prompt Reference

## Monthly Reviews
- "Run my business monthly review"
- "Give me my business brief"
- "What business actions do I need to take this month?"
- "Run my business synthesis"

## P&L
- "Build my P&L summary"
- "What is my revenue this month?"
- "What are my top expenses this month?"
- "What is my profit margin?"
- "How am I tracking against my monthly revenue goal?"
- "Compare revenue this month to last month"

## Revenue & Pipeline
- "Build my pipeline summary"
- "What deals are in progress?"
- "What is the total value of my active pipeline?"
- "Review my business pipeline"
- "What proposals are outstanding?"

## Invoices
- "Log an invoice: [client, amount, due date]"
- "What invoices are overdue?"
- "Flag any overdue invoices"
- "What is my outstanding receivables total?"

## Expenses
- "Log an expense: [amount, category, description]"
- "What are my recurring business subscriptions?"
- "What did I spend on contractors this month?"
- "Categorize my expenses for last month"

## Compliance
- "Check my compliance status"
- "What is my annual report due date?"
- "Has my BOI report been filed?"
- "What compliance deadlines are coming up?"
- "Run my compliance review"

## Contractors
- "Who are my active contractors?"
- "Which contractors will need a 1099?"
- "What have I paid [contractor] year to date?"

## Open Loops
- "Update my business open loops"
- "What business action items are unresolved?"
