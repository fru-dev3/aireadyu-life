# AI Ready Life: Health — Prompt Reference

## Weekly & Monthly Reviews
- "Run my health weekly review"
- "Run my health monthly review"
- "Build my wellness summary for this month"
- "Give me my health brief"

## Labs & Anomalies
- "Review my latest labs"
- "Are any of my lab values out of range?"
- "Build a lab summary from my recent results"
- "Flag any anomalies in my recent health data"
- "Compare my labs this quarter to last quarter"

## Medications & Refills
- "Check my medication refill dates"
- "Which medications are running low?"
- "Flag any medications that need refilling this month"
- "What medications am I currently taking?"
- "Add a new medication to my list: [name, dose, frequency]"

## Preventive Care
- "Am I up to date on preventive care?"
- "What screenings am I overdue for?"
- "Flag any preventive care gaps in my profile"
- "When is my next annual physical due?"
- "Schedule a reminder for my dentist appointment in 6 months"

## Wearable & Wellness Data
- "Sync my Oura Ring data from this week"
- "How is my sleep trending?"
- "What is my HRV trend over the past 30 days?"
- "Am I hitting my step goal this week?"
- "Build a wellness summary from my wearable data"

## Insurance & HSA
- "What is my current deductible progress?"
- "How much is in my HSA?"
- "Am I on track to max my HSA this year?"
- "What does my insurance cover for [procedure]?"
- "Review my health insurance details"

## Appointments & Providers
- "Log a visit note from my appointment today"
- "Who are my current providers?"
- "Remind me to schedule a follow-up in 3 months"
- "What was the outcome of my last PCP visit?"

## Open Loops
- "Update my health open loops"
- "What health items are unresolved?"
- "Mark the refill for [medication] as resolved"
