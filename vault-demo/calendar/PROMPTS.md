# AI Ready Life: Calendar — Prompt Reference

## Weekly Agenda
- "Build my weekly agenda"
- "What's on my calendar this week?"
- "Run my weekly calendar review"
- "What does next week look like?"
- "What are my top priorities this week?"

## Deadlines
- "What deadlines are coming up in the next 30 days?"
- "Collect all deadlines from my active domains"
- "Flag any approaching deadlines"
- "Add a deadline: [name, date, domain]"
- "What is due this month?"

## Focus Time
- "How much focus time did I have this week?"
- "Analyze my focus time for this month"
- "Am I getting enough deep work time?"
- "Review my focus time vs. meeting load"
- "Schedule a focus block for tomorrow morning"

## Deadline Planning
- "Help me plan around the Q2 tax deadline"
- "What should I do this week to hit [deadline]?"
- "Run my deadline planning review"
- "What needs to happen before [date]?"

## Alerts
- "Alert me about any deadlines within 7 days"
- "Flag any approaching deadlines I might miss"
- "What should I put on my radar for next week?"

## School & Family
- "What school events are coming up?"
- "Are there any school closures or breaks I should know about?"
- "When is the next school holiday?"

## Integrations
- "Add this event to my Google Calendar: [details]"
- "Sync my calendar deadlines to Notion"
- "Pull the deadline list from my tax domain"

## Open Loops
- "Update my calendar open loops"
- "What calendar action items are unresolved?"
