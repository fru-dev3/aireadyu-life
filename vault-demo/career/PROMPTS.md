# AI Ready Life: Career — Prompt Reference

## Weekly & Monthly Reviews
- "Run my career weekly review"
- "Run my career monthly review"
- "Give me my career brief"
- "What should I focus on in my career this month?"

## Compensation & Market
- "What is my comp gap vs. the market?"
- "Build my comp summary"
- "What is my total comp?"
- "Flag any comp gap compared to market benchmarks"
- "What should I be earning at my experience level?"

## Market Scan
- "Scan the market for roles matching my profile"
- "What jobs match my target titles?"
- "What is the job market looking like for [target role]?"
- "Are there roles open at [company]?"
- "Review my pipeline of target roles"

## Applications
- "Log a job application: [company, role, status]"
- "What applications are in progress?"
- "Review my application pipeline"
- "Who should I follow up with today?"
- "Draft a follow-up email for my application at [company]"

## Skills Gap
- "What skills should I be developing for my target role?"
- "Run my skills gap analysis"
- "Compare my skills to [target title] requirements"
- "Build a skills gap summary for my career goals"

## Network
- "Who in my network should I reach out to?"
- "Run my network review"
- "Draft an outreach message to [name] about [topic]"
- "Who do I know at [company]?"

## Offers & Negotiation
- "Help me evaluate this offer: [details]"
- "How should I negotiate this offer?"
- "What is the total value of this offer?"
- "Compare my current comp to this offer"

## Open Loops
- "Update my career open loops"
- "What career action items are unresolved?"
