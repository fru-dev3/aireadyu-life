- "Run my document audit"

## Expiring Documents
- "What IDs are expiring in the next 12 months?"
- "Check my expiring documents"
- "Flag any IDs expiring within 6 months"
- "Is my passport valid for the next 12 months?"
- "When does my Global Entry expire?"
- "Flag if my TSA PreCheck is expiring soon"

- "What subscriptions am I paying for?"
- "What subscriptions renew this month?"
