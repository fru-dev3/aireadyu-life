# arlive-vision-log-milestone

**Cadence:** As-achieved (logged whenever a meaningful milestone is reached)
**Produces:** A new milestone record in `vault/vision/00_goals/milestones.md` and a positive signal update to the vision open loops file.

## What it does

Records significant life achievements to the vault so they can be surfaced in scorecard scoring, quarterly reviews, and the annual retrospective. A milestone is anything meaningful enough to remember and worth celebrating — publishing a product, reaching a financial target, completing a learning goal, signing a tenant lease, resolving a long-standing open loop, achieving a health metric, or any other concrete accomplishment across any domain. Each milestone entry captures: the domain it belongs to (career, wealth, health, estate, content, etc.), a short descriptive title, a detailed description of what was achieved and why it matters, the date achieved, and any relevant notes (what made it possible, what it unlocks, what comes next). Milestone records accumulate in `vault/vision/00_goals/milestones.md` as a running chronological log. The monthly scorecard reads this log to count milestones per domain per month, which contributes 20% of the domain score. The annual review reads the full year's milestone log to build the achievements section of the year-in-review. Beyond the mechanical scoring function, the milestone log serves as a personal record of real progress — a counterweight to the open-loops lists that can make the default view feel like an endless backlog.
